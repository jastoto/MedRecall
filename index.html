      Backup & Restore

      Your doctor list and visit history live only in this browser. Back them up regularly so you don't lose them if Safari clears site data, you switch phones, or you delete the app.

      Privacy

      Transcription runs entirely on your phone using an open-source Whisper AI model loaded into the browser — your recording is never uploaded anywhere for this step. Transcripts are only sent to Google Drive if you explicitly choose that save destination.

      Recording consent

      Recording laws vary by state/country — some require everyone on a call to consent. Consider letting your provider know you're recording, especially for phone calls.
